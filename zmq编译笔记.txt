vs2010编译须知

1、额外的h或hpp文件放入include目录
2、Project > Property > C/C++ > General > Additional Include Dictionaries 
添加：C:\Program Files\ZeroMQ 3.2.3\include
3、Project > Property > Linker > General > Additional Library Dictionaries 
添加：C:\Program Files\ZeroMQ 3.2.3\lib
4、Project > Property > Linker > Input > Additional Dependencies
添加：c:\Program Files\ZeroMQ 3.2.3\lib\libzmq-v100-mt-gd-3_2_3.lib
5、复制lib文件对应版本的dll文件到输出exe目录中
